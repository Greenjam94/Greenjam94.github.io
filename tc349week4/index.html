About Me

I first got interested with computers when I was in middle school, watching movies where the hackers broke their way past firewalls to do anything they wanted, steal money, cripple goverments, or save the world. In high school I took a Visual Basic course and a HTML design course... and let's just say I found the difference between Hollywood magic and real programming.

Now I am in college, standing tall with my fellow Spartans and pursuing an education in Computer Science. I've come a long way from coding apps that belonged on a Windows 95 machine; now I have two years of real development under my belt and experience in a multitude of languages.

My Goals

First and foremost, I want to get into Computer Security. I might not be crippling goverments with a few keystrokes, but I still want to earn the title of Hacker. Another long shot goal I have is to earn a lot of money from this, Computer Science has an expanding job field but I also want more than a yearly salary. Hopefully I can create an addicting app or start a service that people just can't live without. Until then I can just keep learning, keep asking questions. I'll never know everything about code, but there's always something I can pick up and apply.
