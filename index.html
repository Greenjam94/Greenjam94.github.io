Github Repo More Information

Devlopement on KORA

Kora was built in the 1990s and was growing as it developed. There's been some major changes along the way but there were some road blocks we (as developers) couldn't get around. You see, KORA was originally built strictly as pages and functions, while it was in PHP, there was no class structure or infrastructure of any kind.

This is why we are taking the fundamentals of Kora, stripping away everything else and added a little Laravel to the mix. Matrix will be bringing you the new and improved KORA in the coming years.
