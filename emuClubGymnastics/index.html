Welcome to EMU Gymnastics Club

Join our club! No experience necessary, just a desire to learn and enjoy gymnastics.
We have opportunities for both men and women in competitive or recreational gymnastics.

Practices

There are practices Monday, Wednesday, and Friday nights 6:30pm - 9:30pm at the Warner Gym C.

Dues

$75 for your first semester or $45 for a second continued semester for practicing.
$175 required for competitive team members, as well as a practice commitment.
